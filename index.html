Welcome to Signature Healthcare Limited

Signature Healthcare Ltd is a locally incorporated private company, leading in marketing of pharmaceuticals, surgical disposables and diagnostic kits. We strive to increase the accessibility and affordability of key, quality products by increasing our reach nationally and throughout Africa. In short we believe everyone has the right to a long and healthy life.

We believe our customers are pivotal to our success. It is this belief, together with our experience, that drives our company forward despite facing increasing competition. In an industry that is constantly changing, our priorities are, and have always been the same; to find and provide innovative products that effectively address the healthcare needs of our nation.
